About WorkSetu

WorkSetu is designed to help daily-wage workers like helpers, masons, electricians, and painters find jobs safely and easily. We connect workers directly with employers, removing the need for middlemen and reducing fraud.

Our mission is to empower workers with technology and create a trusted platform where finding work is simple, fast, and secure.
